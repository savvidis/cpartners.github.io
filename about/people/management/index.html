Meet the executive management team at Pontoon

 

Global President: Michael Wachholz

As Global President of Pontoon, Michael Wachholz has years of experience in the recruitment and staffing industry, having previously served as COO of Beeline, a contingent workforce solutions business unit of Adecco Group.  Prior to joining Beeline, Mr. Wachholz was the CEO of a successful technology and services company, and previously served as an investment banker and a corporate/securities attorney. 

 

Global CFO and Head of Operations: Nicole Burth

Nicole Burth joined Pontoon after serving as CFO of Adecco Solutions since 2010 and holding positions in the M&A, operations and investor relations departments of Adecco Group.  Nicole Burth joined the Adecco Group in 2005 and previously worked as an investment banker with a focus in the business service industry.

 

APAC President: Rishi Kapoor

For the past 15 years Rishi has served as a seasoned executive with an excellent understanding of outsourcing solutions across industry sectors and an international track record of successfully supporting clients and operations in both emerging and established markets. 

 

Senior Vice President, Business Development
(Global MSP) - the Americas: Karin Selfors-Thomann

Joining Pontoon in 2013, Karin Selfors-Thomann is leading the charge for Managed Service Provider sales and business development for the Americas region. Karin previously served as Head of Investor Relations for the Adecco Group since 2008, following executive level positions in the global banking and investment industry.

 

Senior Vice President, Business Development
(Global RPO) - the Americas: Lydia Wilson

With over 20 years of experience in the staffing industry, Lydia Wilson offers a dynamic outlook towards sales and management for recruitment process outsourcing services delivered by Pontoon. She has been in her current role since June 2010, after serving as Senior Vice President with the Adecco Group for nearly 15 years.

 

Head of Sales - EMEA: Tom Mason

Tom Mason recently joined Pontoon to lead our sales effort in the EMEA region. With an expertise in designing and delivering diverse talent sourcing models across the EMEA region, Tom was a partner in a workforce management firm, following leadership roles in talent management for well-known organizations in the workforce solutions industry.

 

Executive Vice President, Global Delivery (Global RPO):
Mike Drolet

Mike Drolet has been leading Pontoon in operational excellence since it’s inception in 2012. Prior to his current role, Mike was the Vice President of Operations for Beeline, an Adecco Group business unit, after serving in operation and service management roles for a large technology firm.

 

Senior Vice President , Global Delivery (Global MSP): Buffy White

With years of experience in relationship management for the workforce solutions industry, Buffy White has taken charge of program management on a global scale for Pontoon since 2012. Previously she served in senior-level implementation and service delivery roles for the Adecco Group for several years.

 

Senior Vice President, Business Analytics: David Pesante

David Pesante has been leading the way in business analytics for Pontoon since it’s launch. Prior to taking on this role, David held senior-level positions for Product and Business Development for VMS partner, Beeline, for nearly 15 years, as well as other roles in the technology industry.

 

Vice President, Human Resources: Terri Lewis

A leader in the human resources industry, Terri Lewis joined Pontoon in 2013 to oversee employee relations on a global scale. Terri held the role of Senior Director for a medical organization prior to joining Pontoon, as well as several HR positions in the manufacturing and retail industries.

 

Vice President, Controller (North America): Klaus Geiser

Klaus Geiser has held the role of financial controller for Pontoon's North America business since it's launch. Prior to this position, Klaus held several key financial positions for parent company, Adecco, over a span of nearly 10 years.
