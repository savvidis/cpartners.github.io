Anyone can claim their people are key differentiators from their competitors. However, at Pontoon we invest heavily in our people to ensure they are not only the smartest in the industry, but they are provided the tools they need to succeed. From their first day on the job throughout their tenure at Pontoon, our people are trained and certified in all aspects of talent management to enable them to be up to speed with best-in-class practices. Our account management team possesses a diverse array of experience in recruitment, procurement, human resources, project management, and customer service, while our shared services team boasts expertise in finance, tax, labor law, change management, and technology. With more than 650 colleagues worldwide, everyone at Pontoon shares the passion for innovation, collaboration, and customer satisfaction.

“I am proud to lead Pontoon because of our people. We have the right dynamic, drive and expertise to build a global organization that will rewrite the rules of talent management.” –Mike Wachholz, Pontoon President
