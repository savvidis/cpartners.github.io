Pontoon Strengthens Their EMEA Leadership Team

JACKSONVILLE, Fla.-- (June 10, 2014) -- Pontoon, the global leader in workforce solutions, announced today several key appointments for its EMEA leadership team as well as a role expansion for the organization’s global CFO.

Strengthening the EMEA leadership team begins with the appointment of Tom Mason, Head of Sales, in EMEA. Mr. Mason, based in London, will report to the Global President, Michael Wachholz, and will have responsibility for Managed Service Provider (“MSP”) and Recruitment Process Outsourcing (“RPO”) sales throughout EMEA. In his role, Mr. Mason will direct European business efforts and administer the business development strategy for Pontoon, assuring that the workforce solutions provider maintains its leadership position through a focus on innovative technology, customer service and total talent management solutions for large and emerging companies within the region. Mr. Mason’s considerable industry experience includes more than 15 years in key leadership roles having previously served as Managing Director RPO EMEA for both Allegis Talent2 and Hudson Highland Group.

To further fortify its market leadership position, Adrian Cross will also join Pontoon. Mr. Cross will serve as the Senior Vice President of EMEA Operations and will also report to Mr. Wachholz. Mr. Cross will be instrumental in leading operational development and delivery of RPO programs in EMEA. Mr. Cross has the distinction of successfully serving hyphen, an Adecco Group company, for over 13 years, and was the 4th employee hired by hyphen. Mr. Cross has performed a wide array of roles within hyphen, managing the operations and implementation functions prior to his appointment into the Chief Operating Officer role in 2011.

“We are very excited to welcome Mr. Mason and Mr. Cross to Pontoon, and look forward to leveraging their extensive experience in further developing our RPO and MSP solutions. I am delighted to have them both join our leadership team,” said Mr. Wachholz.

Finally as part of Pontoon’s EMEA alignment, Global CFO, Nicole Burth will expand her role and she will assume additional leadership responsibilities, including client delivery and relationship management. Based in Zurich, Ms. Burth joined Adecco Group in 2005 and served as a valuable asset in the M&A, operations and investor relations departments, as well as CFO of Adecco Solutions prior to her appointment as CFO of Pontoon. Ms. Burth previously worked as an investment banker with a business service industry focus.

“Nicole’s expanded role better aligns us to capitalize on her expertise and experience in global workforce solutions,” said Mr. Wachholz. “I am confident that these leadership appointments will enable us to become more nimble, more collaborative and ultimately deliver even more value to the organizations we serve.”

Pontoon is located on the web at www.pontoonsolutions.com.

About Pontoon

Pontoon is a market leader in global talent management that provides a competitive advantage through people. Along with unrivaled access to data and workforce intelligence, Pontoon delivers global workforce solutions that reduce risk, increase quality and improve visibility and forecasting—while also reducing cost. Headquartered in Jacksonville, FL, USA and with office locations globally, Pontoon is a wholly owned Adecco company. Pontoon delivers outsourcing solutions in the areas of contingent labor, SOW management as well as employee recruiting and placement in over 70 countries for nearly 100 clients, a global footprint that is broader and deeper than the industry has experienced to date.
