Pontoon Ranked #4 Enterprise RPO Provider by HRO Today

2013 HRO Today Magazine RPO Baker's Dozen Customer Satisfaction Survey

Tweet

JACKSONVILLE, FL (September 24, 2013) – In its annual client-satisfaction survey SharedXpertise, the publishers of HRO Today Magazine ranked Pontoon as the #4 RPO provider out of a field of more than 30 RPO providers who were referenced.  The ranking, announced on Thursday, September 19, 2013 placed Pontoon #4, with an overall score of 584.       

The survey assessed RPO providers in the areas of (1) breadth of service, (2) deal size, and most importantly, (3) quality of service. This year, more than 900 individual responses were received, making the survey the largest body of research performed in the RPO market. In addition to ranking the top enterprise RPO organizations, HRO Today separated customer responses from Europe and the Asia Pacific region to generate distinct lists for those regions. Survey responses from healthcare provider companies were also amassed to produce HRO Today's first ever industry-specific list for healthcare RPO in which Pontoon ranked #8.

Elliot Clark, CEO of SharedXpertise, said, "We received more than 900 surveys from a total of 458 companies, making the 2013 Baker's Dozen the largest RPO customer satisfaction study in the HR trade media."

The RPO Baker’s Dozen is a critical source for prospective buyers of RPO solutions when selecting an RPO service provider, and 2013 marks the ninth consecutive year that Pontoon has been ranked most recently placing #4 in 2013. This demonstrates Pontoon’s commitment to delivering world-class RPO services to a growing portfolio of domestic and multinational clients.

“We are excited when our team is recognized for our achievements and innovative value-adds that deliver proven results for our clients,” said Michael Beygelman, RPO President, Pontoon.  “Our entire team extends a special thank you to our growing portfolio of clients, who reward us every day with their enduring trust and loyalty.” 

Pontoon has continued to demonstrate its commitment towards a portfolio of more than 100 local, regional, global clients, by driving innovation and bottom line results. With the newly announced Pontoon brand, which combines MSP and RPO operations within one organization, Pontoon has emerged as the clear global leader for integrated workforce solutions as one of the largest worldwide MSPs, and has been selected to the HRO Today Baker’s Dozen list for 9 consecutive years. For additional information about Pontoon, and RPO products and services please visit http://pontoonsolutions.com/workforce-solutions/rpo.

About Pontoon

Pontoon, with corporate headquarters in Jacksonville, Florida, United States, is the global leader in workforce solutions. Pontoon specializes in Contingent Workforce Solutions, Recruitment Process Outsourcing and Workforce Consulting. Operating with more than 800 colleagues worldwide, Pontoon currently delivers talent management solutions in 74 countries for its 100-plus clients, a global client base that is broader and deeper than the industry has experienced to date. Pontoon is one of the largest worldwide MSPs, and has been selected to the HRO Today Baker’s Dozen list for 9 consecutive years.
