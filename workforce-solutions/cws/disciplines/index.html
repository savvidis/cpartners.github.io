Pontoon’s experience supports the notion that key to deployment is a comprehensive short- and long-term change management strategy that integrates people, process and technology. Pontoon is well versed in ensuring that minimal disruption occurs and we have the experience to roadmap a smooth and seamless transition that maintains business continuity.  Because each client is different, we create a deployment and change management plan specific to each client’s existing processes and future program goals.

Supply base

Critical to the implementation of a successful supplier model within an MSP are a few components:

  • Equality and Integrity. We ensure the engagement of suppliers is equal and maintains integrity.
  • Standardized Rules. Suppliers are all required to contract similarly, adhering to the same Rules of Engagement, practices and standards and are managed to a consistent financial schedule.
  • Alignment and Optimization. Suppliers are aligned to specific lines of business, geographies, and/or skill sets during the implementation based upon their capabilities, and this is revisited throughout the engagement to ensure optimum utilization.
  • Education and Communication. We develop relationships from day one with each supplier as they are a critical stakeholder in the success of the solution. We deliver training and support equally to ensure all suppliers are set up for success.
  • Performance Standards. We establish measurable performance targets and manage all suppliers to the same standards.

Cost containment

Pontoon is committed to delivering an aligned program with enhanced processes and cost containment. Cost savings are a continuous focus; we work with clients to assess and identify cost savings opportunities associated with an MSP, knowing that this impacts the future success of both of our organizations. Our defined programs can result in significant cost savings with clients during the first year and beyond.

Cost savings are derived from driving cost out of the process through hard cost savings, soft cost savings and cost avoidance. Decentralized clients have the biggest upside regarding cost savings opportunities through program adoption, executive endorsement, process standardization and utilization of the VMS, MSP program analytics, and consolidated invoicing and supplier payment.  

Risk mitigation

Pontoon’s Compliance Management focuses on contract, legal and client policy adherence.  As part of implementation, we work to understand and define policy, controls and pre-work requirements (including background, reference checks, drug tests, etc.). We design the solution and configure the VMS with contracts, training, and on-boarding and off-boarding requirements to support client-specific policies.

We utilize our research specialists and labor law experts to ensure we are updated on regulations, legislation and jurisdictions pertaining to constantly evolving labor and industry-specific areas. We audit ourselves and our suppliers as to ensure compliance.

Visibility & analytics

Clients receive more than just “history” with our solutions. We provide a proactive approach with comprehensive reporting and analytics that include recommendations for ongoing program value. Pontoon is changing behavior through Business Analytics and Actionable Intelligence by not limiting our data to what is obtained in the VMS. We have our own standard templates and methodologies that are driven from Pontoon best practices.

Best practices

More than just a “buzzword” at Pontoon, benchmarking and establishing best practices are paramount to our business objectives.  Our process begins with the collective experience and expertise of our organization, but it does not end there.  We utilize measurement and analysis to challenge ourselves regularly, our workflow, and our systems to find improvement.
