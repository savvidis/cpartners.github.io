Pontoon has developed a management structure for globalizing MSP solutions that delivers benefits targeting the business needs, objectives, change management and market conditions specific to each client’s culture and environment.  In general, the benefits of implementing a global MSP program are: process efficiencies, quality improvement, risk mitigation, visibility, and cost management for a greater return on investment.

  • Process Efficiencies.  Implementing a global program utilizing a single VMS platform provides for an enterprise-wide program supported by Standard Operating Procedures, centralized management and consistent forms with local compliance.
  • Quality Improvement. Pontoon’s streamlined end-to-end lifecycle management not only improves efficiencies, but also resource quality, cycle time and turnover rates. Using scorecards to manage and optimize our supply base, Pontoon can quickly identify non-performing suppliers and workers for all locations and take corrective action immediately.
  • Risk Mitigation. Pontoon’s MSP program shields our clients from financial and regulatory risk through oversight, expertise and compliance management. All Pontoon MSP programs provide strict governance and risk mitigation through extensive compliance assessment of all operations. Our expertise in global delivery provides added value through our legal team’s knowledge of legislation and regulations specific to each country. Through our aggregate view of third-party relationships, we ensure proper classification of resources for each site and IP control for all locations.
  • Visibility. Even if in-country laws prohibit direct oversight of operations, the VMS can capture the information, allowing total transaction visibility. Using reliable real-time data and our business analytics, we monitor SLAs and KPIs for detailed analysis across all client locations. We know who is working where, for how much, for how long, for whom, and why (which project). We then evaluate how this compares to the local market, and when necessary, Pontoon will recommend adjustments specific to each location.
  • Cost Management. Global visibility allows Pontoon to recommend hard and soft dollar savings. Through benchmarking we standardize supplier fee structures ensuring a fair (not excessive) supplier profit, which also allows us to identify and eliminate rogue or maverick spend. We also reduce over-hiring with proper alignment of manager needs/worker ratios to market rates. Based on a complete view of your business, Pontoon will be able to advise you on current and future state opportunities for a great ROI on your contingent workforce.
