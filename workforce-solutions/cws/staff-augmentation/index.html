Pontoon has implemented our Staff Augmentation and Services Procurement Managed Service Provider (MSP) solutions in more than 70 countries. This represents an industry evolution through our expertise and our ability to deploy every possible combination of services, allowing us to continually meet or exceed our client’s requirements. Every client need is different; therefore, every MSP has unique components that must be considered when building the solution. We are confident that there is no requirement we have not already met or cannot implement. Our implementation team and methodology is second to none in the industry and is designed to address the complex scenarios and combinations of solutions that our clients face every day. We are astutely aware of how critical it is that the MSP solution be flexible and we build a solid program foundation that is consistent and easily replicated to expand enterprise-wide.

At Pontoon, we expect to build a custom MSP for each client. A solution that meets their specific objectives, encapsulates their strategic focus, and aligns with their resource needs.  Staff Augmentation strategies are always unique, and only Pontoon has the depth and breadth to construct a tailor made solution that is both cost effective and on strategy.  Our MSP approach is simple… Meet the clients objectives, be it a global solution with a single PMO and VMS, or multiple PMOs and VMS tools, Pontoon has that expertise. 

Vendor neutral / vendor preferred

Our approach to achieve a high performing MSP is to ensure the right suppliers are engaged, aligned to the right resource requirements in the right place at the right time.  Contrary to some vendor neutral models where all requisitions or POs go to all vendors simultaneously, Pontoon engages suppliers based upon predetermined business logic including capabilities and geography as well as candidate pipeline—and suppliers are heavily weighed on previous performance.  

Payroll services

Pontoon is capable of assisting Clients in achieving high quality, global payroll solutions. Operating as a vendor neutral MSP, we have strategic partnerships with firms that specialize in Payroll Services, and we can work directly with our client’s preferred providers. During implementation we partner with clients to analyze their unique footprint; volume and locations, and determine the appropriate best-in-class providers to optimize their needs.  Pontoon can also provide payroll services in partnership with sister organizations in the Adecco Group.
