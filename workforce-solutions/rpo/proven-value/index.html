Recruitment Process Outsourcing is the primary focus


Pontoon is a recognized leader in Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO), working with over 100 global clients to maximize their recruiting efforts.  Offering clients a choice, Pontoon can serve as a partial or full-service recruitment arm that manages the team, process and technology for an organization’s hiring needs.  Holding true to the client’s employment strategy, Pontoon executes the day-to-day recruitment operations and ongoing enhancements to optimize effectiveness   

Recruiting Challenges and Solutions

Pontoon has a proven record of analyzing the myriad of challenges related to recruitment processes then delivering a customized resolution.

Challenge: candidate sourcing in a competitive market

Sourcing candidates for “hard-to-fill” jobs is always a challenge in a competitive market.  From IT/MIS and key sales positions, to engineering and technical roles, top talent is currently employed, well paid, and happy.  To attract and capture that talent Pontoon goes beyond the typical “post and pray” strategy with a true competitive recruitment strategy employed by recruiters with deep sourcing expertise.  

  • Faced with the problem of sourcing the most sought after individuals in a highly competitive market, Owens-Illinois turned to Pontoon to develop and execute a sustainable recruitment strategy.  Pontoon helped them grow their R&D department by almost 50%.  By hiring only top-level research scientists, many with PhD qualifications, the Owens-Illinois Patent in Intellectual Property filings grew nearly ten-fold in the first two years.

Challenge: elevated recruitment costs

Reducing recruitment costs without compromising quality is a common challenge.  Pontoon leverages investments in technology, staff, training, and infrastructure to reduce costs without sacrificing quality of service.  Pontoon delivers significant savings by coupling buying power that lowers technology and sourcing vendor rates with the elimination of wasteful agency and search fees.

  • In a single year, Pontoon reduced Morgan Stanley’s recruitment costs by $6 million by implementing a direct recruiting strategy for hard-to-fill positions that reduced the need to pay expensive search firm fees.

Challenge: prolonged hiring process

With companies operating leaner than ever, each vacant job is a critical one that can lead to lost days and ultimately lost revenue.  Pontoon's process improvement expertise shaves critical days—often weeks—off an organization’s average time to hire.  A proprietary parallel recruiting process moves candidates through the hiring process faster with committed service levels (SLAs) guaranteeing speed at each step.  Plus, proactive pipelining means pre-screened candidates can be ready in hours instead of weeks.  

  • Within the first year of implementing a Pontoon RPO solution focused on process streamlining, Allison Transmission saw a 20% decrease in their time to fill metric. In year two, Time-to-fill was reduced even further to a 30% decrease.

Challenge: high turnover rate

New hire attrition costs money and lowers morale.  Pontoon identifies the best-fit candidates who are most likely to be productive in their new position and happily stay with an organization long-term.  Pontoon, prior to hire, assesses the behavioral and cultural match between the candidate and the organization, provides the candidate with a realistic job preview, then determines aptitude for the position to be filled. 

  • Gate Gourmet, faced with extremely high attrition rates, came to Pontoon to perform a recruitment audit.  It was determined that Gate Gourmet suffered from poor employment brand/image issues and was attracting the wrong types of candidates.  Pontoon developed and implemented a solution that brought the Gate Gourmet interview-to-hire rate from 7:1 to 1.5:1.  New hire attrition dropped from nearly 50% to approximately 20%.   In the first year alone, the quality of their candidates improved so much they hired over four times as many candidates per submitted slate.

Challenge: need a scalable recruitment function

Seasonality, mergers and divestitures, economic forces, and changes in business strategy all conspire to demand an ever-greater degree of flexibility in an organization’s recruitment function.  One month there may be 100 jobs to fill, followed by 200 the next month.  Pontoon's recruitment infrastructure is scalable allowing for expansion and reduction of activities to meet changing needs on a moment’s notice.  Pontoon’s costs scale with service due structured outsourcing arrangements that reduce the need to pay for a larger-than-needed recruitment function during slow times or paying huge ramp-up costs when things get busier. 

  • With a large, seasonal hiring push in the first quarter of each year, Banfield Pet Hospitals was in need of a scalable, cost-varied recruiting solution. Annually, Pontoon screens over a 1,200 applicants and places 350 veterinary students into their student summer jobs program. Throughout the remainder of the year, the program contracts to a much smaller level, but every spring when it is time to increase hiring, their processes are ready to be executed
