Our Supplier Mission Statement

In our goal of becoming the industry’s “MSP of choice” to our Supplier partners, we will continue to develop a Supplier community servicing the full breadth of our Clients’ talent needs, capitalizing on proven performance of Suppliers. At Pontoon, it is our pledge to be committed to establishing strong partnerships with our clients. This commitment extends to our Suppliers as well. Our vast Supplier network enables us to establish win-win scenarios that maximize both Supplier and client returns on investment. The Pontoon approach to Supplier management is strategic in that we translate performance data to actionable information, thereby allowing our most successful partners to grow with us.
