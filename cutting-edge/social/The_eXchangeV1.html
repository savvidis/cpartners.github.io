Bonjour Pontoon

Pontoon recently launched in France.  Through the re-branding of Adjust HR, the Pontoon family added 60 colleagues and an important business line.  Pontoon France operates within the second largest global market for temporary labor.

Through a collaboration between the French and global marketing teams, Pontoon successfully re-deployed the French website (pontoonsolutions.fr), our first non-English language site, as well as a wealth of marketing collateral; brochures, email templates, Powerpoint templates, flyers and business stationary. The French site, a sub-site within pontoonsolutions.com will also serve as a model for future language translations globally.

Since launch, pontoonsolutions.fr has already enjoyed an 888% increase in traffic from French speaking visitors, and the French landing page is already a top 10 destination among all of pontoonsolutions.com site pages.

The launch of Pontoon in France was celebrated by the entire team.  They “wore their branding on their sleeves” and enjoyed the company of Michel Stokvis, President Pontoon EMEA and Nicole Burth, Global CFO during the day’s festivities.

pontoonsolutions.com—Our Award Winning Website 

After only a few short months, pontoonsolutions.com is already being recognized as an industry-leading website. Recently, our site was honored by two separate organizations for its outstanding content, design and functionality.

pontoonsolutions.com was recently awarded the 2013 Interactive Media Award for excellence in website design and development. The site was designated as Best in Class in the recruiting category. The award was created by the Interactive Media Council, a nonprofit organization of leading web designers, developers, programmers, advertisers and other web-related professionals.

According to competition rules, “the Best in Class award is the highest honor bestowed by the Interactive Media Council. The winner of this award has excelled in all areas of the judging criteria and achieved a perfect, or near perfect, overall score. This site represents the highest standards of professionalism, standards compliance and impeccable planning and execution.”

Judging criteria includes: Design, Content, Feature Functionality, Usability, Standards Compliance and Cross-Browser Capability. Out of a possible score of 500, pontoonsolutions.com scored an impressive 488.

In May 2013, it was announced that Pontoon is a top three finalist in the Best Overall Web Design category for the 2013 American Business Awards. pontoonsolutions.com will ultimately be selected as a Gold, Silver, or Bronze Stevie® Award winner during the 11th annual Stevie® Awards ceremony on Septmeber 16 in San Francisco. Other finalists include: Carnival Cruise Lines, Life Lock and the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants. We are already thrilled to be recognized among such outstanding organizations; however, as Nicole Burth, Pontoon CFO, has taught us… “Pontoon is going for gold.”

More important than awards, pontoonsolutions.com is hosting more and more visitors each month—customers and prospects, industry experts as well as Pontoon colleagues. Since our launch on January 7, 2013, we have seen site traffic increases each month.  

We have several exciting updates and enhancements scheduled for pontoonsolutions.com this summer, so please continue to visit our site – our award winning site.  For a complete list of Pontoon awards, visit our online trophy case.

RPO Industry Recognition

Human Resources IQ and The Outsourcing Institute presented two awards at the HR Shared Services & Outsourcing Summit in Chicago. The awards were established to honor, recognize and promote HR shared services and RPO providers that set standards of excellence within the HR shared service industry.

Lydia Wilson attended, and accepted The Outsourcing Institute's first annual client satisfaction award of RPO providers, which ranked Pontoon as the Number One provider of RPO services.  Thank you to the entire Pontoon team of colleagues and associates dedicated to RPO for their dedication and focus on exceptional customer service.

Pontoon's Client Advisory Board

The Pontoon US MSP Client Advisory Board held its Launch meeting at the Sawgrass Marriott in Ponte Vedra Beach, Florida. The Client Advisory Board is a select group of client executives that provide the voice of the Pontoon client community to influence our strategy and roadmap. All ten members of the Board were invited to attend, and six members attended the meeting.

The agenda was designed to encourage collaboration and inspire an organic conversation. The objective was not for Pontoon to present to the client members, but instead offer suggestions for discussion and let client members contribute to most of the dialogue. Conversations addressed many hot topics affecting businesses today, with a focus on workforce and talent issues. Board members also enjoyed a peek into our roadmap, which was largely validated by their feedback.

Pontoon Introduces Gamification to the Recruiting World

A groundbreaking instructional presentation followed by interactive fireworks was delivered at the HRO Today Forum in Philadelphia by Kevin Werbach, professor at The Wharton School and co-author of For the Win: How Game Thinking Can Revolutionize Your Business and Michael Beygelman, Pontoon President North America and Global Practice Leader RPO.  Werbach, a devout World of Warcraft player who led the Obama administrations review of FCC policy, shared his cutting edge insights into play as work and revealed how game thinking can motivate employees and customers alike.  Beygelman, RPO industry leader then electrified the audience by plugging in his phone and showing live gaming enabled recruitment.  Pontoon is using this innovation for internal recruitment.  Visit the Pontoon website to see our first quiz game and test your knowledge.  We aim to refresh our recruitment gaming community regularly to ensure that we stay engaged with top talent.  See for yourself how Pontoon is deploying ground-breaking innovation in recruitment.
