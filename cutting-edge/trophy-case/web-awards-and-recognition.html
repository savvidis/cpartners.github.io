Pontoons wins Gold Stevie® Award in 2013 American Business Awards SM

Pontoon was presented with a Gold Stevie® Award in the Best Overall Web Design –Business Services category in The 11th Annual American Business Awards. The American Business Awards are the nation’s premier business awards program. All organizations operating in the U.S.A. are eligible to submit nominations – public and private, for-profit and non-profit, large and small. For details, click here.

 

 

 

Pontoonsolutions.com named Best Consulting Website in the 17th annual WebAward competition for Web site development

More than 1,500 entries from 40 countries were adjudicated in 96 industry categories during this year's competition. Entries were judged on design, copy writing, innovation, content, interactivity, navigation, and use of technology. Pontoonsolutions.com received an overall score of 65 points, with the average industry score listed as 49 points. Pontoonsolutions.com Awarded for Web Site Design Excellence

In April 2013, pontoonsolutions.com was awarded a 2013 Interactive Media Award for excellence in website design and development. The site was designated as Best in Class in the Recruiting category. The award was created by the Interactive Media Council, Inc. (IMC), a nonprofit organization of leading web designers, developers, programmers, advertisers and other web-related professionals. 

According to competition rules, “the Best in Class award is the highest honor bestowed by the Interactive Media Council. The winner of this award has excelled in all areas of the judging criteria and achieved a perfect, or near perfect, overall score. This site represents the highest standards of professionalism, standards compliance and impeccable planning and execution.”

Judging criteria includes: Design, Content, Feature Functionality, Usability, and Standards Compliance and Cross-Browser Capability. Out of a possible score of 500, pontoonsolutions.com scored an impressive 488. For more information about the award, visit Interactive Media Awards.
