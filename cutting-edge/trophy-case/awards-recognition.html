Pontoon Named Number One RPO Provider in Client Satisfaction Survey

Pontoon has recently been named as the number one RPO Provider based on client-satisfaction as part of the 2013 “10 Best” RPO Provider Ranking survey. The survey was conducted by The Outsourcing Institute (OI), in association with Human Resource Executive (HRE) Magazine and the International Quality & Productivity Center (IQPC). Based on the responses of the clients of RPO Providers, the data was collected and analyzed across four primary categories: understanding client needs, solution delivery, value for fees and general service capabilities. The scoring methods used resulted in Pontoon as the overall top-ranked provider, with Pontoon ranking #1 and #2 for “solution delivery” and “general service capabilities” respectively.
